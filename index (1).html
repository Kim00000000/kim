Huricans and Storms

Location: Hurricane is a storm that happens in the Atlantic Ocean and northeastern Pacific Ocean while Typhoon happns in the northwestern Pacific Ocean.

Occurring Date: Hurricane occurs between August to November but occurring dates of Typhoon are concentrated in May to October.

Meaning and Origin of words: The word hurricane comes from the Taino Native American word, hurucane, meaning evil spirit of the wind. the word typhoon comes from Chinese which means giant wind.
